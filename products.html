Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)? expand_more

Our standard minimum order quantity for international vessel shipments is 12,500 metric tons. For containerized shipments, we can accommodate smaller volumes starting from 10 containers (approx. 250 MT).

What payment terms do you accept? expand_more

We typically work with Irrevocable, Transferable or Non-Transferable Letters of Credit (DLC/SBLC) from top 25-50 world banks. Specific terms can be negotiated based on trade history.

Can you provide ICUMSA 45 from Brazil? expand_more

Yes, Brazil is one of our primary sourcing origins for ICUMSA 45. We have direct relationships with major mills in the Santos region to ensure consistent supply and competitive pricing.
