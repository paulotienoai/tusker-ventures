Frequently Asked Questions

What is the required documentation for Inventory Financing? expand_more
Typically, we require warehouse receipts from a Tusker-approved collateral manager, current financial statements (Profit/Loss & Balance Sheet), and valid export/import licenses for the specific commodity.
How quickly can I draw down on an approved line? expand_more
Once the facility is active, individual draw-downs are processed within 24 hours. For automated trade credit, approval is instantaneous at the point of transaction on our marketplace.
Do you offer multi-currency disbursement? expand_more
Yes, we currently support USD, EUR, GBP, and SGD for financing facilities. We also offer currency hedging services to protect against exchange rate volatility during the tenure of the loan.
