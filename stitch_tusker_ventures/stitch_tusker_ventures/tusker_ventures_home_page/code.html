Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our trading and financing processes.

What is the minimum trade volume? expand_more

Our typical physical trades start at 500 metric tons for containerized shipments and 12,500 metric tons for break-bulk vessels. However, we offer flexibility for long-term strategic partners.

How do you handle price volatility? expand_more

We utilize exchange-traded derivatives (Futures & Options) to hedge our physical positions. We can offer fixed-price contracts or floating-price contracts with caps and floors to suit your risk appetite.

What financing instruments do you accept? expand_more

We primarily work with Irrevocable Letters of Credit (LC), SBLCs, and Cash Against Documents (CAD). For established clients, we offer deferred payment terms and open account structures.
