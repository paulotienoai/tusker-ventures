Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about sugar trading and project financing.

What types of sugar grades do you facilitate?

expand_more
We specialize in various grades including ICUMSA 45 (White Refined Sugar), ICUMSA 150 (Crystal Sugar), and VHP Raw Sugar. Our platform provides real-time pricing and quality certifications for each batch.

How does the trade financing model work?

expand_more
Tusker Ventures offers structured trade finance solutions tailored for agricultural cycles. This includes pre-export financing, letter of credit facilitation, and inventory-backed lending to ensure smooth cash flow for both producers and buyers.

Is your platform available for small-scale farmers?

expand_more
Absolutely. We provide a 'Cooperatives Program' that allows small-scale farmers to aggregate their output, access better market rates, and utilize our risk management tools which were previously only available to large-scale estates.

How do you ensure transparency in the supply chain?

expand_more
We use integrated blockchain-verified traceability for every shipment. From the plantation to the final warehouse, every stakeholder can track the origin, quality testing results, and logistical status in real-time.
