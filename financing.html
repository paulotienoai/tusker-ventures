Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our capital and trade finance approach.

Why do you require pre-qualification? expand_more
Pre-qualification ensures mutual fit. We want to work with partners who share our values and meet our criteria for responsible capital deployment. This process protects both parties and establishes a foundation of trust from the beginning.
What makes your approach different from traditional lenders? expand_more
We're relationship-first, not transaction-first. We take time to understand your business, your goals, and your challenges. Our structures are designed for long-term partnership, not just short-term profit. And our faith-based principles guide every decision we make.
What happens after I complete pre-qualification? expand_more
If you meet our initial criteria, you'll be invited to a consultation call with our team. This is an opportunity for both parties to learn more about each other and explore potential partnership structures. From there, we'll work together on due diligence and deal structuring.
Do you work with startups or new businesses? expand_more
Our financing programs require a minimum of 2 years operational history. However, we're always open to conversation. If you have a compelling story, strong backing, or unique circumstances, please reach out to discuss. We evaluate each situation individually.
