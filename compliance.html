Documentation Requirements

Know Your Customer (KYC)

Standard documentation required for all new trading relationships.

business

For Companies (KYB)

  • check_circle
    Certificate of Incorporation

    Official company registration documents

  • check_circle
    Memorandum & Articles of Association

    Company bylaws and governance documents

  • check_circle
    Board Resolution

    Authorization to conduct commodity trading

  • check_circle
    Beneficial Ownership Declaration

    Identification of ultimate beneficial owners (25%+)

  • check_circle
    Director ID Documents

    Passport/ID copies for all directors

  • check_circle
    Bank Account Details

    Corporate bank account confirmation letter

  • check_circle
    Tax Registration Certificate

    VAT/GST or equivalent tax ID

person

For Individuals

  • check_circle
    Valid Government ID

    Passport, national ID, or driver's license

  • check_circle
    Proof of Address

    Utility bill or bank statement (within 3 months)

  • check_circle
    Source of Funds Declaration

    Origin of trading capital documentation

  • check_circle
    Bank Reference Letter

    Letter from banking institution

  • check_circle
    Professional References

    Trade references from previous partners

Note: Individual traders typically operate through registered business entities for commodity transactions.
