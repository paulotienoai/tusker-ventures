What is ICUMSA-45?

ICUMSA is a measurement standard for sugar purity and color. ICUMSA-45 is commonly associated with refined, high-grade sugar often used in global trade.

The number 45 refers to the color of the sugar on the ICUMSA scale - the lower the number, the whiter and more refined the sugar. ICUMSA-45 is the highest quality refined white sugar available in the international market.
